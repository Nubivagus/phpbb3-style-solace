Adding more presets

Solace has 6 Preset Styles by default. You can easily add your own presets by modifying one file: 

solace/presets.yaml

Let's take a look at Preset 6: 


preset6:
  accent: "#3E342A"
  accent2: "#ececec"
  accent_light: "#3E342A"
  forumicon_background: "#ffffff"
  forumicon_background_unread: "#3E342A"
  forumicon_color: "#3E342A"
  forumicon_color_unread: "#ffffff"
  main_background: "#ffffff"
  main_text_color: "#555555"
  headers_text_color: "#898989"
  main_border_color: "#ececec"
  navbar_background: "#3E342A"
  navbar_text_color: "#ffffff"
  error: "#BC2A4D"
  contrast: "#000000"
  code: "#2E8B57"
  footer_image_background: "backgrounds/preset6.jpg"

As you can see, it's just a set of key variables with values. Just copy whole set, change preset count to preset7, adjust variables and you're done:
