Introduction to Configuration

In Solace style, configuration is handled by phpBB 3.1 administration extension called Devlom Configurator. Installation process of Devlom Configrator was covered in first chapter.

To configure Solace style, go to phpBB Administration Control Panel and click Extensions tab. Go to Devlom Configurator section and click "Settings". You should be greeted by Devlom Configurator Colours section.

In next chapters we're going to describe each Devlom Configurator section. Due to the fact that every section's option is described in the extension itself, we're going to focus on most important parts only.

Very Important ! After saving your settings in Devlom Configurator, you might need to delete board cookies in the forum to see applied changes. To do that, open your forum and press Delete all board cookies link in the bottom navbar.
